<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Cryostats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bath Cryostats</li><li>Closed-Cycle Cryostats</li><li>Multistage Cryostats</li><li>Continuous-Flow Cryostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryostats are essential instruments used to achieve and maintain low temperatures for various applications. Bath cryostats utilize a thermally insulated space filled with a refrigerant to cool samples, providing stable temperature conditions. Closed-cycle cryostats recycle helium gas through a compression and expansion process, eliminating the need for continuous gas supply. Multistage cryostats employ multiple cooling stages for achieving ultra-low temperatures. Continuous-flow cryostats circulate a cryogenic fluid, allowing for more efficient cooling and temperature stabilization over extended periods.</p></p>
