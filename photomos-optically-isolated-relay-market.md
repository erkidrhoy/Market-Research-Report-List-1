<p><p>PhotoMOS Optically Isolated Relays are electronic switching devices that employ optoelectronic technology to provide electrical isolation and signal transmission without direct electrical contact. They consist of a light-emitting diode (LED) and a photodetector, utilizing light to switch the relay mechanism, thus ensuring electrical isolation between input and output circuits. This technology offers advantages such as low power consumption, high reliability, and resistance to electrical noise, making them ideal for various applications in telecommunications, industrial automation, and consumer electronics.</p><p>The PhotoMOS Optically Isolated Relay Market is anticipated to expand significantly in the coming years, driven by increasing demand for automation across industries and the growing emphasis on energy-efficient solutions.
