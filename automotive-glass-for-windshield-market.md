<p><ul><li>Tempered</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive glass for windshields market primarily involves three types: tempered glass, laminated glass, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and shatter resistance, making it ideal for side and rear windows. Laminated glass consists of a sandwich of two glass layers with a plastic interlayer, offering superior safety by preventing shattering and minimizing injury during accidents, commonly used for windshields. Other types may include acoustic and tinted glass, catering to specific performance and aesthetic needs.</p></p>
