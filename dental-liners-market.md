<p><strong>The Dental Liners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Ionomer</li><li>Calcium Hydroxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental liners market primarily includes two main types: glass ionomer liners and calcium hydroxide liners. Glass ionomer liners are known for their biological compatibility, adhesion to tooth structures, and fluoride release, making them suitable for both cavity lining and as a base. Calcium hydroxide liners, on the other hand, are favored for their excellent antimicrobial properties and ability to stimulate reparative dentin formation. Both types play crucial roles in protecting the pulp and enhancing overall dental restoration outcomes.</p></p>
