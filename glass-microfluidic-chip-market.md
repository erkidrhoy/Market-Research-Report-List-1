<p><p>The glass microfluidic chip market comprises various types catering to diverse applications. Straight channel chips facilitate fluid flow along defined paths, ideal for simple mixing and analysis. Chamber chips feature enclosed compartments for more complex reactions and biological assays. Droplet generator chips create tiny fluid droplets for high-throughput experiments, enabling precise control of reactions. Meander chips utilize serpentine channels to enhance mixing and reaction times, making them suitable for various biochemical processes. Each type serves distinct functionalities in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
