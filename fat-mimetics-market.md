<p><p>The Fat Mimetics market is categorized into three main types: carbohydrate-based, protein-based, and lipid-based. Protein-based mimetics leverage proteins to provide creaminess and improve satiety without added fat. Lipid-based mimetics, derived from fats or oils, offer a similar flavor profile and texture as traditional fats, but with reduced caloric content, appealing to health-conscious consumers seeking lower-fat alternatives.</p></p>
