<p><p>Sortation systems are essential in logistics and warehousing, optimizing the process of sorting packages for efficient distribution. Linear sortation systems operate in a straight-line configuration, typically used for high-speed sorting tasks with fewer sorting destinations. In contrast, loop sortation systems utilize a circular track design, allowing for more flexible operations and handling of varying package sizes and shapes. Both systems enhance productivity and accuracy in sorting processes, catering to different operational needs in the supply chain industry.</p></p>
