<p><ul><li>Atherosclerosis</li><li>Myocarditis</li><li>Pericarditis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inflammatory heart disease encompasses conditions like atherosclerosis, myocarditis, and pericarditis. Atherosclerosis involves the buildup of plaque in coronary arteries, leading to reduced blood flow and potential heart attacks. Myocarditis is the inflammation of the heart muscle, often caused by infections, affecting the heart's ability to pump efficiently. Pericarditis is the inflammation of the outer layer of the heart, which can cause chest pain and affect heart function. Each type presents unique challenges and treatment opportunities in the market.</p></p>
