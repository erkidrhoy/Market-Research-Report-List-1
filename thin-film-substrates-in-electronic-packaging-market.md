<p><p>Thin film substrates in electronic packaging refer to the materials used to support and interconnect electronic components. Rigid thin-film substrates are characterized by their solid structure, providing stable support for high-density circuits, often used in applications requiring durability and precision, such as LED displays and medical devices.
