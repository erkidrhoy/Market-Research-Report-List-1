<p><strong>The X Ray Flat Panel Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Type</li><li>Direct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray flat panel sensor market comprises two primary types: indirect and direct type sensors. Indirect type sensors convert X-rays into visible light before capturing the image, typically using a scintillator. This method offers high sensitivity and image quality. In contrast, direct type sensors convert X-rays directly into electrical signals, allowing for quick image acquisition and reduced dose exposure. Both types are used in medical imaging, with their selection depending on specific imaging requirements and clinical applications.</p></p>
