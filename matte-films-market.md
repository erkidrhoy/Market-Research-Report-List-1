<p><p>The matte films market is segmented into several types based on material composition. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) films are known for their durability and clarity, suitable for graphics and packaging. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) films offer flexibility and are resistant to abrasion, often used in protective applications. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) films are lightweight and economical, commonly employed in labeling and packaging. PE (Polyethylene) films provide moisture resistance and are used in various packaging applications. The "Other" category encompasses additional specialized materials.</p></p>
