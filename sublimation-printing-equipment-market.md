<p><p>The sublimation printing equipment market encompasses three main types: printers, heat presses, and consumables. Printers are specialized devices that transfer dye onto materials, achieving high-quality images. Heat presses are machines that apply heat and pressure to transfer the dye from the printed paper to the substrate, ensuring durability and vibrancy. Consumables include the inks, transfer papers, and substrates used in the process, essential for achieving optimal printing results. Together, these components drive innovation and growth in the market.</p></p>
