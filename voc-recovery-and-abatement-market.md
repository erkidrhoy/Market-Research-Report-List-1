<p><p>The VOC recovery and abatement market comprises several technologies aimed at controlling volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Oxidizers convert VOCs into harmless byproducts through combustion. Filtration systems capture particulates and VOCs, enhancing air quality. Membrane separations use selective barriers to separate VOCs from air streams. Scrubbers and strippers remove VOCs via liquid absorption, while condensation captures VOCs by cooling air. Adsorbent systems utilize materials that attract and hold VOCs, improving emission control and promoting environmental protection.</p></p>
