<p><p>The stationary sandblasting machine market is divided into two primary types: dry and wet. Dry sandblasting machines use compressed air to propel abrasive materials onto surfaces, making them suitable for quick, efficient cleaning and coating removal. In contrast, wet sandblasting machines mix water with the abrasive, reducing dust and minimizing environmental impact while providing a smoother finish. Both types cater to various industries, including manufacturing and construction, based on specific project requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
