<p><strong>The Oral Contraceptive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combination</li><li>Progestin Only</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral contraceptive market is primarily categorized into three types: combination pills, progestin-only pills, and others. Combination pills contain both estrogen and progestin, providing effective pregnancy prevention and additional benefits like regulating menstrual cycles. Progestin-only pills, also known as mini-pills, are suitable for women who cannot use estrogen due to health concerns. The "others" category includes specialized formulations, such as extended-cycle or low-dose options, catering to diverse consumer preferences and medical needs, enhancing accessibility and choice in contraception.</p></p>
