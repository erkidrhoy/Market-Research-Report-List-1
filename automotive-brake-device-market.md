<p><p>The automotive brake device market encompasses various technologies designed to enhance vehicle safety and control. ACC (Adaptive Cruise Control) maintains speed and distance from other vehicles. CMBS (Collision Mitigation Braking System) reduces the risk of collisions by automatically applying brakes. EBS (Electronic Brake System) offers precise braking force control. EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution) optimizes brake force among wheels for stability. ESP (Electronic Stability Program) enhances vehicle control during skids, while VSA (Vehicle Stability Assist) helps maintain traction during slippery conditions.</p></p>
