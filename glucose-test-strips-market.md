<p><p>The glucose test strips market is segmented into three main types: glucose oxidase test strips, glucose dehydrogenase test strips, and other glucose testing methods. Glucose oxidase test strips utilize the enzyme glucose oxidase to convert glucose into gluconic acid and hydrogen peroxide, facilitating color change for measurement. Glucose dehydrogenase test strips employ glucose dehydrogenase, allowing for a more specific reaction that is less affected by oxygen levels. Other categories include alternative technologies and innovations, reflecting diverse testing needs and preferences in diabetes management.</p></p>
