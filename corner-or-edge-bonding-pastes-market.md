<p><p>Corner or edge bonding pastes are essential in electronics assembly, especially for securing components at edges or corners. The market is divided into two main types: reworkable and non-reworkable. Reworkable pastes allow for adjustments or replacements after initial application, enabling easier repairs and modifications. In contrast, non-reworkable pastes cure to a permanent bond, providing robust adhesion but without the option for rework. Both types serve specific needs based on production processes and end-use requirements in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
