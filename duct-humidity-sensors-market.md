<p><strong>The Duct Humidity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Sensor</li><li>Resistance Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The duct humidity sensor market comprises primarily two types: capacitive sensors and resistance sensors. Capacitive sensors measure humidity by detecting changes in electrical capacitance caused by moisture levels, offering high accuracy and quick response times. Resistance sensors, on the other hand, rely on changes in electrical resistance based on humidity, typically providing a simpler and cost-effective solution. Both types serve essential roles in HVAC systems by ensuring optimal moisture control, enhancing energy efficiency, and improving indoor air quality.</p></p>
