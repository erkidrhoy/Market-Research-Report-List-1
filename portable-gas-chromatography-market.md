<p><strong>The Portable Gas Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-solid chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable gas chromatography market is primarily categorized into gas-solid chromatography (GSC) and gas-liquid chromatography (GLC). GSC utilizes a solid stationary phase, allowing for the separation of gases based on adsorption characteristics, commonly used in environmental monitoring and industrial applications. In contrast, GLC employs a liquid stationary phase, facilitating the analysis of volatile compounds in various samples, often used in food safety, pharmaceuticals, and petrochemicals. Both types enhance field analysis capabilities with portability in diverse settings.</p></p>
