<p><p>The processed meat fat analyzers market is primarily divided into two types: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) technology and NIR (Near-Infrared) technology. DEXA technology uses X-rays to accurately measure fat content and distribution within meat products, providing detailed insights for quality control. NIR technology, on the other hand, leverages near-infrared light to quickly assess fat levels based on absorbance patterns, making it suitable for rapid testing in production environments. Both technologies enhance meat quality assessment and ensure compliance with health standards.</p></p>
