<p><ul><li>Insulating Glass</li><li>Tempered Glass</li><li>Low-e Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial glazing market comprises several key types, including insulating glass, tempered glass, and low-emissivity (low-e) glass. Insulating glass features double or triple glazing, enhancing energy efficiency and thermal performance. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. Low-e glass is coated to reflect infrared light while allowing natural sunlight, improving energy savings and reducing heat transfer. Together, these products cater to diverse architectural needs while promoting sustainability and safety.</p></p>
