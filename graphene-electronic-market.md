<p><p>The graphene electronic market comprises various types, including Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) graphene, which is produced by depositing carbon atoms from a gas onto a substrate, resulting in high-quality films. Graphite-exfoliated graphene involves mechanically or chemically peeling graphite layers to obtain graphene, offering a cost-effective alternative with varied properties. Other methods encompass liquid-phase exfoliation and reduction of graphene oxide. Each type serves distinct applications in electronics, sensors, and composites, driving innovation in advanced materials.</p></p>
