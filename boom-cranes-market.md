<p><p>Boom cranes are essential lifting devices utilized in various industries, with two primary types being knuckle boom cranes and telescopic boom cranes. Knuckle boom cranes feature a jointed arm, allowing for greater flexibility and precision in lifting and placing loads in tight spaces. In contrast, telescopic boom cranes extend and retract using multiple sections, offering impressive reach and height capabilities, making them ideal for heavy lifting tasks. Both types cater to diverse applications, enhancing efficiency in construction, logistics, and industrial operations.</p></p>
