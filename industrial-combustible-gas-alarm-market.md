<p><ul><li>Catalytic Bead (CB) Type</li><li>Dot/non-dispersive Infrared (PIR/NDIR) Type</li><li>Electrochemical</li><li>Semiconductor Type</li><li>Laser Type</li><li>Open Circuit Infrared (OPIR) Type</li><li>Ultrasonic Gas Leak Detection (UGLD) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Combustible Gas Alarm market comprises various technologies for detecting gas leaks. Catalytic Bead (CB) Type sensors use heat produced from gas oxidation for detection. Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) type sensors measure gas concentration via infrared light absorption. Electrochemical sensors detect specific gas concentrations through chemical reactions. Semiconductor types utilize changes in electrical resistance. Laser detection provides precise gas measurements using light scattering. Open Circuit Infrared (OPIR) employs IR beams for wide area monitoring, while Ultrasonic Gas Leak Detection (UGLD) identifies leaks by detecting sound waves produced by escaping gas. Each type serves specific industrial safety needs.</p></p>
