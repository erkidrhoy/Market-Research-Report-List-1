<p><p>The Wire Insulation and Jacketing Compounds market is primarily classified into two categories: Halogenated and Non-Halogenated plastic compounds. Halogenated plastic wire insulation includes materials like PVC, known for their flame-retardant properties, but may release harmful gases when exposed to fire. Conversely, Non-Halogenated plastic insulation, often used for environmentally sensitive applications, utilizes alternatives that do not emit toxic halogen gases, offering enhanced safety and reduced environmental impact. These distinctions cater to varying industry needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
