Brown seaweed, known for its high iodine content and alginate, is commonly found in fertilizers and health supplements. Green seaweed, often used in salads and as snacks, contains essential nutrients and is gaining popularity in health food sectors. Each type has unique applications and market potential, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
