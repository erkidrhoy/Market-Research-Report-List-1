<p><p>The commercial vehicle coolant market encompasses three main types: ethylene glycol, propylene glycol, and glycerin. Ethylene glycol is widely used due to its effective heat transfer properties and affordability, but it poses toxicity risks. Propylene glycol is a safer alternative, non-toxic and environmentally friendly, making it popular in applications where safety is a concern. Glycerin, derived from natural sources, is biodegradable and suitable for eco-conscious users. Each type has unique properties catering to different commercial vehicle requirements and regulations.</p></p>
