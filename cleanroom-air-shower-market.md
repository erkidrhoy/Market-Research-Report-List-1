ULPA filter air showers, on the other hand, capture 99.999% of particles as small as 0.12 microns, offering a higher level of filtration for ultra-sensitive environments. Both types ensure cleanliness and contamination control, essential for maintaining sterile conditions in various industries.</p></p>
